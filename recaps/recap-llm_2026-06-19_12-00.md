## CONTEXT
The app runs in two modes: standalone Electron (IPC via preload.js) and client mode (Express server
at electron/server.js proxies all IPC-like calls as REST endpoints). Storage module was added in
commit b32a85a with IPC handlers but its routes were missing from server.js. ReceiptPreview.jsx
footer showed server-local time and could silently show "—" when session.full_name was empty.
